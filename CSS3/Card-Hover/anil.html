Kro moskevska, designed by Neuhausl Hunal, is an interior design project featuring a prominent green theme, reminiscent of a nearby football stadium.

The fourth and most recent establishment in the KRO series of bistros and cafés opened in Prague’s Vršovice district.

The clean, basic layout consists of classic, flexible tables for two. The primary lighting works in a similar way, with a grid of flush-mounted spotlights.

The material palette is dominated by profiled dark green heraklith on the ceiling, complemented by green tiling. Besides the bar and the foot rail, the tiling is fully showcased in the restrooms.
